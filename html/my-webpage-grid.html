Historical Places

District Six Museum

District Six Museum was formed in 1989. On 10 December 1994 the Museum exhibition called Street:Retracing District Six. The Museum is a member of SAMA(South African Museum Association) and of ICSOC(International Coalition of Sites of Conscience).

Iziko Museum

The Iziko South African Museum is a South African national museum located in Cape Town. Iziko is an IsiXhosa word meaning 'hearth'. Iziko Museum operates 11 national museum in Cape Town.

Iziko Slave Lodge

The Slave Lodge was built in 1679. The slave lodge was the largest slave holding at the Cape until 1806. The building was renamed the slave lodge on Heritage Day, 1998.
